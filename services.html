Transportation service

A driver, bearing a sign with your name on, will pick you up and will securely drive you to our hotel anytime, day or night.This is an affordable alternative to local taxi services, and is offered by Olive Green for a small fee of 16 EUROS.

Hotel breakfast

Our hotel offers an abundant breakfast buffet with a wide choice of dishes. Breakfast at Montserrat hotel is considered one of the very best in the European, with more than 100 different dishes and beverages. Guests may enjoy live piano music with their breakfasts every Saturday and Sunday.

Fitness room

Fitted with the latest equipment and located in a luxurious and modern complex with fine ocean views, our fitness center provides the basis of a healthy and relaxing stay. Our suite of pool, jacuzzi, is the perfect antidote to a long day spent in the city.
